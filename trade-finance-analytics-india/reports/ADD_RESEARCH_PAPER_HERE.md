# PLACEHOLDER — Add Your Research Paper Here

## What belongs in this folder:

### `research_paper.pdf`
Your academic research paper on Factoring and Forfaiting.

To add it:
1. Export or save your paper as `research_paper.pdf`
2. Copy it into this `reports/` folder
3. Run: `git add reports/research_paper.pdf`

### Note
If your paper is not yet published or is a draft, you can name it:
- `research_paper_draft.pdf`
- `factoring_forfaiting_india_study.pdf`

Update the filename reference in README.md accordingly.
